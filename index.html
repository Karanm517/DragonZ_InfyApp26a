Pitch

DriveQuest is an interactive learning app designed to help new and teenage drivers become safer and more responsible on the road.

Instead of being a boring handbook, the app turns driving education into an engaging experience that helps users actually enjoy learning important road safety rules.

We built DriveQuest because many new drivers lack experience and awareness, which can lead to dangerous situations. Our goal is to reduce accidents by encouraging better decisions behind the wheel.
